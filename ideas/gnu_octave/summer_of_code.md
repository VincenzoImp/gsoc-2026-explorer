# Summer of Code

[Jump to navigation](https://wiki.octave.org#mw-head)

[Jump to search](https://wiki.octave.org#searchInput)

Octave has participated in the [Google Summer of Code](https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/) (GSoC) since 2011 (as part of GNU in 2011-2013 and 2015) and the [ESA Summer of Code in Space](https://step.esa.int/main/esa-socis-2016/) (SOCIS) since 2012. Both programs continue to bring potential new contributors to Octave and have them work on interesting projects that could be merged into Octave or otherwise benefit the Octave community.
